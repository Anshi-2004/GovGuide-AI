INCOME CERTIFICATE APPLICATION

Purpose of Income Certificate:
- Apply for scholarships
- Get admission fee waiver
- Apply for government schemes
- Educational loan subsidy
- EWS category reservation

Who Issues Income Certificate:
- Tehsildar
- Sub-Divisional Magistrate (SDM)
- Revenue Officer
- District Magistrate office

Eligibility:
- Should be resident of the state
- Annual family income should be verifiable
- All income sources must be declared

Documents Required:
- Ration Card
- Aadhaar Card of all family members
- Salary slips (if employed)
- Income Tax Returns (if applicable)
- Agricultural land records (if farmer)
- Self-declaration affidavit
- Residence proof
- Family photograph

Application Process:
Step 1: Visit Tehsil/Revenue Office OR apply online through state portal
Step 2: Fill application form with family details
Step 3: Attach all required documents
Step 4: Submit at counter or upload online
Step 5: Collect acknowledgement receipt
Step 6: Verification by revenue inspector
Step 7: Certificate issued by Tehsildar

Online Application:
- Visit state revenue portal
- Register with mobile number
- Fill income certificate form
- Upload scanned documents (under 200KB each)
- Submit application
- Note application number
- Pay fee online (if applicable)

Offline Application:
- Download form from district website
- Fill manually or type
- Attach self-attested documents
- Submit at Tehsil office
- Collect acknowledgement slip

Verification Process:
Step 1: Revenue Inspector Visit
- Inspector visits your residence
- Verifies family members
- Checks income sources
- Prepares field report
- Timeline: 7-10 days

Step 2: Tehsildar Review
- Reviews inspector's report
- Cross-verifies documents
- Approves or rejects application
- Timeline: 5-7 days

Common Rejection Reasons:
- Incomplete family details
- Income source not verifiable
- Mismatch in address proof
- Previous income certificate not expired
- Document quality poor/unclear
- Family member details missing
- Income exceeds eligibility limit

Validity:
- Valid for 6 months to 1 year (state-specific)
- Must be renewed after expiry
- Cannot be used after validity period

Timeline:
- Online application: 15-30 days
- Offline application: 20-40 days
- Urgent cases: 7-10 days (with extra fee)

Fee Structure:
- General category: ₹20-50
- SC/ST category: Free or ₹10
- Urgent processing: ₹100-200

How to Check Status:
- Visit state revenue portal
- Enter application number
- View current stage
- Download certificate if approved

What to Do If Rejected:
- Check rejection reason in portal
- Correct the issue (missing documents, wrong info)
- Reapply with corrections
- Can file complaint if rejection is wrong

Income Calculation:
Include:
- Salary from all employed family members
- Agricultural income
- Business income
- Rental income
- Pension

Exclude:
- Loans
- One-time receipts
- Gifts
