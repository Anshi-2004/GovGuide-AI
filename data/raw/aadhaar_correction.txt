AADHAAR CORRECTION SYSTEM

Types of Corrections Allowed:
- Name correction (spelling mistakes)
- Date of birth correction
- Gender correction
- Address update
- Mobile number update
- Email ID update
- Photograph update

Name Correction Process:
Step 1: Visit UIDAI website or myAadhaar portal
Step 2: Login with Aadhaar number and OTP
Step 3: Select "Update Aadhaar Details"
Step 4: Choose "Name" correction
Step 5: Upload supporting document
Step 6: Pay fee (if applicable)

Documents Required for Name Correction:
- Passport
- PAN Card
- Driving License
- Bank Passbook
- Gazette Notification (for major changes)
- Marriage Certificate (for married women)
- School Leaving Certificate

Date of Birth Correction:
Documents Accepted:
- Birth Certificate issued by Registrar
- School Leaving Certificate with DOB
- Passport
- PAN Card (only if DOB is same)

Important Rules:
- Only one DOB correction allowed in lifetime
- Document should be issued before Aadhaar enrollment
- Affidavit alone is NOT sufficient

Address Update:
Documents Accepted:
- Passport
- Bank Passbook (with address printed)
- Electricity Bill (within 3 months)
- Telephone Bill (within 3 months)
- Property Tax Receipt
- Rent Agreement (registered)

Online vs Offline Process:
Online Method (for minor corrections):
- Visit myAadhaar portal
- Upload documents
- Pay ₹50 (if applicable)
- Get URN (Update Request Number)
- Track status online
- Updated Aadhaar delivered in 90 days

Offline Method (for major corrections):
- Visit nearest Aadhaar Seva Kendra
- Carry original documents
- Biometric verification required
- Pay fee at counter
- Collect acknowledgement slip
- Updated Aadhaar sent to address

Common Issues:
Issue: "Document rejected during verification"
Solution: Ensure document is clear, not expired, and name matches exactly

Issue: "Mobile number not registered"
Solution: Visit Aadhaar center with original documents to register mobile

Issue: "Update request rejected"
Solution: Check rejection reason in status, resubmit with correct documents

Issue: "Aadhaar not received after update"
Solution: Download e-Aadhaar from UIDAI website using URN

Verification Timeline:
- Document upload: Immediate
- UIDAI verification: 7-15 days
- Approval/Rejection: 15-30 days
- Aadhaar dispatch: 30-90 days

Fees:
- Address update: Free (once in 10 years)
- Other updates: ₹50 per update
- Biometric update: Free

Track Update Status:
- Visit UIDAI website
- Enter URN (Update Request Number)
- Enter Aadhaar number
- View current status
